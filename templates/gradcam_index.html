üß† What is Grad-CAM?

Grad-CAM (Gradient-weighted Class Activation Mapping) shows you exactly which parts of the image the AI model is looking at when making its prediction.

  • Red/hot areas: Most important for the prediction
  • Blue/cool areas: Less important regions
  • Multiple visualizations for different prediction classes
